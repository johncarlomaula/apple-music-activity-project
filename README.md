# Visualizing My Apple Music Consumption

**Project Decription:** In this project, I visualize my Apple Music listening history from August 2020 - April 2022. I used SQL to organize and clean the raw data from Apple before visualizing the results in Tableau.

For more information, check out my [blog](https://johncarlomaula.github.io/project4_music) to see a summary write-up of the project. You can also view the resulting dashboards from my Tableau Public Account below.

1. **[Listening Activity Dashboard](https://public.tableau.com/views/AppleMusicListeningActivity/AppleMusicListeningActivity?:language=en-US&:display_count=n&:origin=viz_share_link)** - focuses on my music listening habits over time
2. **[Music Breakdown Dashboard](https://public.tableau.com/views/MusicPlayCounts/Dashboard1?:language=en-US&:display_count=n&:origin=viz_share_link)** - focuses on my most played artists, albums, songs, and genre

**Main Goals:**
- Use SQL to organize and clean raw Apple Music data from August 2020 - April 2022
- Analyze and visualize a breakdown of my music consumption
- Analyze patterns in my music consumption over time
